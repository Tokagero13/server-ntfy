NTFY Subscription

To receive notifications on your mobile device, you need to install the NTFY app and subscribe to a topic.

1. Download the NTFY app from the official website.

2. Subscribe to the topic: {ntfy_server}/{topic}

Replace `{topic}` with the desired notification channel.
